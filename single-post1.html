Abu Robocon 2017

Robocon is organised by Asia-Pacific-Broadcasting-Union(ABU), a collection of over 20 countries of Asia Pacific Region.The most awaited event ignites the mind of technocrats of the country with different themes every year. Be it nominal funds or stern scenarios nothing has ever barred our will power.The curiosity to do something sometimes takes us to a path where we even forget to take the meals for the whole day and what not!!! Every drip of sweat shed for the entire year reflects itself in those 3 days.

The most important among all, will always be the last tragic moment when we have to make the statergy according to our competitors and try to do better than others.As competitors,we divide our work such that we can manage to complete our part of work along with it if possibility of changes that can be made is there we never step back and do that with the same swirl and thrill and try our best to come out with flying colours. Getting better each year, 2017 unveils an AIR-6. Aiming high for this year too as our biggest competitors is ourselves.

Robocon 2018 is being hosted by Vietnam with the theme 'NÈM CÓN' in which we are expected to design formidable robots and charismatic shuttlecocks showing their colours. The hard work through robocon has taught us to convert thoughts into reality, team spirit and positivity.The planning made at the tip moment in order to compete with the pack of other technocrats,the patience to create a change in our bot after observing the bots of other technocrats are the things that we learn and apply. Most important of all this is maintaining a good team work to flag the name of VSSUT ROBOTICS at the top will always be our pin pointed goal.
